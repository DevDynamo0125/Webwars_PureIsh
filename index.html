About ICCSAI

The International Conference on
Communication, Security, and Artificial
Intelligence (ICCSAI) is a non-profit
international conference designed to
advance knowledge in Computer &
Communication Engineering. The 2025
edition, ICCSAI-2025, is scheduled to be
held from April 4th to 6th, 2025, at
Galgotias University in Greater Noida, India.
This event is technically sponsored by
the IEEE Uttar Pradesh Section.
